**Brotherhood Under the Bridge** is a title that explores life in extreme poverty and the harsh reality of battling addiction to drugs and alcohol. The game focuses on a deep narrative layer, forcing the player to make difficult moral choices in a world that has long forgotten the meaning of mercy.

Our team is working day and night to deliver a product that combines a high-quality story with engaging gameplay mechanics.

### Key Features:
* **Uncompromising Realism:** A raw look at the lives of the homeless and social outcasts.
* **Psychological Depth:** Gameplay mechanics that reflect the impact of addiction on the protagonist's mind and body.
* **Branching Narrative:** A multi-threaded story where every decision carries weight and consequence.

This game utilizes the modern **Vulkan renderer (Godot 4 Forward+)** to deliver high-fidelity dynamic lighting and atmospheric effects.

**Minimum:**
* **OS:** Windows 10 (64-bit)
* **Processor:** Intel Core i3-4150 / AMD FX-4300
* **Memory:** 4 GB RAM

**Recommended:**
* **OS:** Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
* **Processor:** Intel Core i5 / AMD Ryzen 5
* **Memory:** 8 GB RAM
* **Graphics:** NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1060 (6GB) / AMD Radeon RX 580
* **Storage:** SSD (Recommended for seamless asset loading)
